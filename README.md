# scale_cesd — Center for Epidemiologic Studies Depression Scale

A 20-item self-report scale designed to measure depressive symptomatology in the general population for epidemiological studies. Each item rated 0–3 (rarely/none of the time to most/all of the time) over the past week.

- **Instrument:** CES-D (CES-D) · Depression — Epidemiologic
- **20 items** (see `data/cesd.json`).
- **Scoring:** Range: 0–60. Score ≥16 suggests clinically significant depressive symptoms. Validated across diverse populations; widely used in epidemiologic and clinical research.
